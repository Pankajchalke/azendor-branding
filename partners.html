Managing Partner

A business lawyer, Indranil has a rich and varied experience of over 22 years. Rachna Vats

Partner

+91 9619166364 rachna.vats@azendor.com

Rachna has completed her Master of Laws (LL.M) with a specialization in Intellectual Property (IP), from the University of Mumbai. She has more than 16 years of post-qualification experience which includes experience as an in-house counsel in the media and entertainment industry (Viacom18), and in law firms, where she has independently worked on IP, strategic and commercial contracts. She has done substantial work pertaining to the television broadcasting industry, OTT platforms, technology and other digital business. Having an in-depth knowledge of rights, Rachna has not only managed end-to-end contracts for production, but has also dealt with top licensors across the world for acquisition/licensing of content. She also spearheaded the legal function for launch and management of the OTT service 'Voot'. Her legal and commerce background enables her to strategize, structure, draft and negotiate complex agreements.
